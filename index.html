Built by parents, for parents

Numbat Math is made for U.S. families with kids in grades 3, 4, and 5. The parent is always the legal account holder — kids can't sign up on their own. We don't sell data, we don't run ads, and the AI tutor never sees your kid's name.

This isn't a summer-only app. The summer regression piece is what got us building, but the roadmap goes year-round — STAAR practice for Texas families, state-test prep elsewhere, and ongoing topic packs as our own kids age into them.
